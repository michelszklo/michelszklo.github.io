Michel Szklo

I'm a first-year doctoral student in Public Administration and Government at Fundação Getulio Vargas (EAESP FGV), research assistant at the Center for Politics and Economics of the Public Sector, and founder and executive director at LAPP (Public Policy and Social Programs Lab) -- a platform the seeks to gather, organize and provide in a straightforward fashion, information (causal evidence) on the impacts of public policy and social programs that went under rigorous evaluation worldwide. I also hold a bachelor's degree from the São Paulo School of Economics (EESP/FGV) and a master's degree from Tilburg University, all in economics.

I truly believe that evidence-based decision-making is the key to the development and strengthening of public policies and to positively impact the livelihoods of those who need the most. In my free time, I enjoy listening and playing music and cycling.
